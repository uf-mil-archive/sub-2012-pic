This page provides access to calibration routines for the DUAC. Detailed information regarding each of the calibration procedures is listed in the Support section.

Thrust Curve Calibration

This calibration procedure will power the motor and command it to incrementally traverse its entire voltage range in the forward direction and then the reverse direction while logging data. After the calibration routine has completed, a dump file of the logged data will be available for download.
WARNING: Starting this calibration routine will power the motors.
